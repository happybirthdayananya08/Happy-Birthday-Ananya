Chapter 2 Once Upon a Time…

Once upon a time, on a soft January morning, a princess named Ananya arrived, and the world felt a little kinder as if the clouds themselves had stitched pink ribbons for her coming.

The kingdom remembered her laughter, her brave little heart, and the way she chased sunbeams on the balcony. Little did the world know many secret pages lay folded inside her story, waiting to be revealed by royal magic.
