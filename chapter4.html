Chapter 4 A Hidden Letter

My dearest Ananya,

Today I opened a page and found you a gentle courage wrapped in laughter. I wanted to tell you how proud I am of every small step you take.

When the nights feel long and the books grow heavy, remember this: the stars above still cheer for you, and I do too. You are not alone in any chapter.

With all my heart,
Ishanya
